What We Do

AI That Sees What Your Eyes Miss

FreshScan uses a ResNet50 / EfficientNet deep learning model trained on thousands of labeled images across 28 classes of fruits and vegetables.

It detects subtle visual cues — color changes, texture degradation, and surface irregularities — that indicate whether a food item is still safe to eat.

This system is designed for use in smart agriculture, supermarkets, food logistics, and at home — making AI-powered freshness detection accessible to everyone.
